MY BESTFRIENDS


Sabejon, Rafael C.


This is an essay for all of my friends, who saved me when I needed it.

A best friend is like a star; you don't always see them, but you know they're always there.
I am thankful, because I met them in different situations; they saved me when no one did.
Even though sometimes we don't check on each other, the care is still there when one needed it.
I love the idea that you can run to them when you feel everything is so heavy. You can rant on them.

Without judgement, they accept you for who you are, your imperfections, and your mistakes.
I would like to express my appreciation for their existence.
I love all of them; everyone of them deserve all the happiness in this world
they mean so much to me.
